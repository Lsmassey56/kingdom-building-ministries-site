Unusual Soldiers

The special forces of discipleship training, Unusual Soldiers will take you to the edge of yourself. As a ministry, Unusual Soldiers is committed to taking the Hope of Christ to the dark, dangerous, and despised corners of the world, and to raising up more people to join in the fight.

Unusual Soldiers Programs

Stranded

STRANDED is not your typical event, therefore its not for everybody. It is the “Basic Training” of Unusual Soldiers. This training event is for those that have a passion for God, a desire to impact the world, and an adventurous spirit. This beast called Stranded, will test your endurance, character, and mental fortitude as you engage in a mock mission trip to a dangerous region full of tribesmen, wild animals, and rebels. Stranded keeps you on your toes because the script always changes and sometimes it hurts.

For more information, including dates and pricing, visit unusualsoldiers.com

Slingshot

SLINGSHOT is a training event for those that have been through Submerge or on an Unusual Soldiers Scouting Trip. Slingshot helps students develop a personal strategy on reaching the dark, dangerous, or despised place that God has placed on their heart.
Students are required to bring a laptop and as much information as they can about the area (or people group) that God has placed on their heart.
By the close of SLINGSHOT students will have a full blown strategy on their next steps and how to begin pursuing the vision that God has given them.

For more information, including dates and pricing, visit unusualsoldiers.com
