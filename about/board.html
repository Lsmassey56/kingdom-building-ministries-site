KBM Board

The Kingdom Building Ministries' board is made up of men and women who are active Kingdom laborers. They are committed to seeing more of God and His will and glory come to pass as laborers are raised up. This KBM board roster represents those currently serving God’s call to challenge, equip, and serve people everywhere who are becoming God’s everyday Plan A Kingdom laborers with hearts on fire and lives on purpose.
