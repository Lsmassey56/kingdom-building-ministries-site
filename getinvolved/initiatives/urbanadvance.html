Urban Advance

The Urban Advance Initiative is committed to raising up laborers and laborer reproducing leaders in an ever-increasing multi-ethnic and urban reality. Through training, consulting, resources, and scholarship opportunities, we seek God to use HIS church to bring truth, transformation, justice, and love to the city.

Through seminars, staff trainings and coaching, Efrem Smith is available to aid churches or ministries in developing best practice models for multi-ethnic and urban ministry.

To book Efrem Smith for training or consulting, click here.

Areas of training and consulting include:

  • Reconciliation Development
  • How to develop ministry models and best practices as churches seek to engage urban subcultures and multicultural realities for the Kingdom of God
  • How to develop ministries that are Christ-centered and multi-ethnic
  • How to engage urban subcultures in developing urban ministry geared toward youth, worship and outreach
  • How to equip the next generation of preachers with cross-cultural, character and expository competencies
  • Providing follow-up resources to churchs and other ministries in urban and multi-ethnic contexts.
