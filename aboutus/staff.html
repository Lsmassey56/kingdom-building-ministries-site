KBM Staff

When Kingdom Building Ministries was founded in 1986, God conveyed to Dwight Robertson as the first staff member called to an expanding missional laborership movement, "I will call others, and together you will do it." God’s call over many years has involved countless volunteers, staff members, speakers, financial investors, and prayer workers. This KBM staff roster represents those currently serving God’s call to challenge, equip, and serve people everywhere who are becoming God’s everyday Plan A Kingdom laborers with hearts on fire and lives on purpose.
